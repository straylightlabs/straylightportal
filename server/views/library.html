Equipment and tools (“Utility Wall”)

Straylight maintains a library of shared tools, equipment, and other resources which can be used or borrowed by Straylight members. Many of these items can be found on the utility wall or in storage areas in Straylight One.


If you have interesting tools or items that you don’t mind sharing and may be useful to others, please consider bringing them into Straylight One to be added to the library. All items are registered in an Airtable database to maintain a record of who has loaned each item.


If you would like to borrow an item for external use, or see which items are currently checked out, please follow the links below.

Digital resources

Our digital library contains learning materials, documents, templates, case studies, code, designs, recordings, software licenses, and other digital resources that may be useful to our community.


Many of these resources are byproducts of community projects and efforts (e.g. loan and planning documents for Straylight One, or code for Straylight Connect), while some are privately owned but shared for the general interest of our community (e.g. Continuum project resources).


While many of these resources would be easy to use outside of Straylight, we ask members to be respectful and cautious when making copies or derivative works for personal or commercial use, and when in doubt, please ask the original creator(s) about usage rights and permissions.
